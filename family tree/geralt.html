Geralt of Rivia, forged in trials and tempered by choice, walks the line between monster and man. His story binds them all. A blade for hire, a heart in conflict, Geralt navigates a world that fears what it needs most. Beneath the white hair and wolf medallion lies a soul shaped by loss, loyalty, and reluctant love. He is protector, wanderer, and witness; tied by fate to Ciri, Yennefer, and the brotherhood of Kaer Morhen. In silence and steel, he carries the burden of stories no bard can fully sing.
