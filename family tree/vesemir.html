Vesemir – The Mentor
Home Vesemir Eskel Lambert Geralt Ciri Yennefer

Vesemir, the oldest Witcher, is the guiding hand behind Kaer Morhen’s legacy. He raised and trained the next generation with patience and wisdom. A relic of a fading era, Vesemir carries the weight of history in every scar and story. His teachings shaped warriors, but his presence offered something rarer stability, compassion, and quiet strength. Though the world around him changed, Vesemir remained the anchor, the father figure, and the last flame of an ancient order.
