Yennefer of Vengerberg, fierce and brilliant, is Ciri’s protector and Geralt’s heart. Her magic is matched only by her will. Beneath the elegance and sharp wit lies a survivor shaped by sacrifice, ambition, and a longing for control over her own fate. She commands chaos with precision, yet her greatest strength is the love she chooses to give. In a world of shifting alliances and brutal truths, Yennefer remains unyielding
