Lambert – The Brash Young Wolf
Home Vesemir Eskel Lambert Geralt Ciri Yennefer

Lambert’s sharp tongue hides a fierce heart. Though impulsive, his loyalty to the Witcher brotherhood runs deep. He challenges authority, mocks tradition, and rarely holds back—but beneath the sarcasm is a warrior who never walks away from a fight that matters. Raised in the same crucible as Geralt and Eskel, Lambert’s defiance is forged from pain, pride, and a need to prove himself.
