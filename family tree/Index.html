Dandelion, flamboyant bard and loyal friend, is the voice behind the legends. With lute in hand and wit at the ready, he records the trials, triumphs, and tangled fates of Witchers and their kin. His songs weave sorrow and satire, turning bloodshed into ballads and heartbreak into history. Though never a fighter, his words shape the legacy of those who are. In every tale of Geralt, Ciri, and Yennefer, Dandelion is both witness and chronicler of chaos, all wrapped in rhyme.
