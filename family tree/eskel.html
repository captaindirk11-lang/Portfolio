Eskel – The Loyal Brother
Home Vesemir Eskel Lambert Geralt Ciri Yennefer

Scarred but steadfast, Eskel is Geralt’s closest brother-in-arms. Quiet strength and unwavering loyalty define his path. Though marked by battle and solitude, Eskel carries the weight of Kaer Morhen’s legacy with calm resolve. He speaks little, but his actions echo louder than most, shielding his kin, honoring his mentor, and standing firm when the world turns hostile.
